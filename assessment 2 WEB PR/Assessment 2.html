Welcome to Football World

Football, also known as soccer, is the world’s most popular sport played by millions across the globe. It brings people together with excitement, passion, and team spirit.

History of Football

Football traces back over 2,000 years to ancient China, but the modern game started in 19th-century England. It quickly spread globally, becoming a unifying sport with major leagues and tournaments like the FIFA World Cup.
