The Witcher

A fantasy drama based on the book series by Andrzej Sapkowski, following Geralt of Rivia, a monster hunter in a world full of magic, danger, and political intrigue.

Cast

  • Henry Cavill as Geralt of Rivia
  • Anya Chalotra as Yennefer of Vengerberg
  • Freya Allan as Ciri
  • Joey Batey as Jaskier

Main Characters

Geralt of Rivia, a Witcher navigating a war-torn land, Yennefer, a powerful sorceress, and Ciri, a princess with a mysterious destiny.

Reviews

The Witcher has been praised for its immersive storytelling and world-building, receiving a strong fan following.
