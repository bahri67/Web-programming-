Moroccan Chicken Tagine

A traditional Moroccan dish slow-cooked with chicken, aromatic spices, olives, and preserved lemons. It’s rich in flavor and perfect with couscous.

Ingredients

  • 1 whole chicken, cut into pieces
  • 2 preserved lemons, quartered
  • 1 large onion, finely chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/2 cup green olives
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tsp ground cumin
  • 1 tsp ground coriander
  • 1 tsp ground ginger
  • 1 tsp paprika
  • 1/2 tsp ground cinnamon
  • Salt and black pepper to taste
  • 1/2 cup chicken broth
  • Fresh cilantro for garnish

Method

  1. Heat olive oil in a tagine or heavy-bottomed pot over medium heat.
  2. Add chopped onions and garlic, and sauté until softened.
  3. Stir in the spices: cumin, coriander, ginger, paprika, cinnamon, salt, and black pepper.
  4. Add the chicken pieces and brown them on all sides.
  5. Pour in the chicken broth and add the preserved lemons and olives.
  6. Cover and simmer for about 45 minutes to 1 hour until the chicken is tender.
  7. Garnish with fresh cilantro and serve with couscous or bread.
